About

Building interfaces people genuinely enjoy using — where performance meets craft.

I'm a front-end developer at A&G Insurance Australia in Brisbane, where I architect Angular applications that serve millions of policy holders through partnerships with ING, Virgin, Coles, and Ozicare.

Before Australia, I spent two years in Istanbul building luxury e-commerce for fragrance houses — Majouri, Maison Matine, and the MAA-ALTHAHAB family of brands. That experience taught me that great front-end work is invisible: fast loads, fluid interactions, and layouts that make products look irresistible.

My path started with a Computer Science degree in Lebanon, a CCNA certification, and IT infrastructure work in Abu Dhabi. Each chapter added a layer — networking fundamentals, system thinking, full-stack development, and finally, the front-end specialization I practice today.
