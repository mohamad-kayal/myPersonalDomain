Frequently asked questions

What happens after the 14-day trial?

Your trial includes full Professional plan features. After 14 days, choose a plan to continue. If you don't choose, your account pauses — no charges, no data loss.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you get immediate access to new features. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

Is there a contract or lock-in?

No contracts. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime. Annual plans are paid upfront with a 20% discount and can be cancelled with a prorated refund.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express via Stripe. Enterprise customers can pay by invoice with NET 30 terms.

Can I export my data if I leave?

Absolutely. You own your data. Export everything — inspections, photos, reports, vehicle history — as CSV and PDF at any time. We retain data for 30 days after cancellation.

Do you offer discounts for dealer groups?

Yes. If you manage 3+ locations, contact our sales team for volume pricing. We also offer discounts for annual billing (20% off).
