Getting Started

Visit the signup page and follow the 3-step process: enter your details, set up your dealership, and choose a plan. Your 14-day free trial starts immediately with full Professional features.

Go to Administration > Users and click Invite User. Enter their email and select a role (Inspector, Sales Manager, or Administrator). They'll receive an email invitation to join your dealership.

Go to Administration > Settings > Branding. Upload your logo, set your brand colours, and add your contact information. These will appear on all customer-facing PDF reports.

Click New Inspection from the sidebar or dashboard. Enter the vehicle's VIN (scan or type), then work through the checklist sections. The system auto-saves every 30 seconds so you never lose progress.

Inspections

Enter or scan a 17-character VIN. The system automatically looks up the vehicle's year, make, model, trim, engine, and body type. If the VIN has been seen before, the full inspection history is shown.

Each checklist item has a camera icon. Tap it to capture a photo directly from your device camera or upload from your gallery. Photos are automatically linked to the correct checklist item and appear in the report.

In the Damage Map step, click on the vehicle diagram where damage is located. A form will appear to classify the damage type (scratch, dent, crack, etc.) and severity (minor, moderate, major). Add notes and an optional photo.

Plug an ELM327-compatible Bluetooth OBD-II adapter into the vehicle's diagnostic port. In the inspection, go to the Diagnostics step and click Connect Scanner. The system will scan for fault codes and capture key readings. Note: Web Bluetooth works in Chrome only.

Reports

Once an inspection is approved, open the inspection report and click Download PDF. You can choose between an internal report (full details) or a customer-facing report (branded and curated).

Click the Share button on any approved report. You can generate a unique shareable link (with optional expiry date) or email the report directly to the customer. The customer does not need an account to view the report.

Administration

Go to Administration > Templates. You can edit existing templates or create new ones. Each template can have custom sections and items. You can set items as mandatory or optional, and choose between different vehicle types.

Go to Profile > Billing & Plan. From there you can upgrade or downgrade your plan, update your payment method, and view billing history. Changes take effect immediately for upgrades or at the end of your billing cycle for downgrades.

Go to Analytics and click the Export button to download inspection data as CSV. For a full data export (all inspections, vehicles, photos, reports), contact support and we'll prepare a complete export package within 24 hours.
